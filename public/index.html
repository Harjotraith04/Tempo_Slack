Tempo

An executive-function co-pilot for Slack. It triages the firehose, remembers what you promised, decodes what people actually meant, and protects your attention.

Add to Slack

What it does

  • Triage — sorts a week away into Act / Blocker / FYI / Noise, grounded in real messages.
  • Commitment Ledger — surfaces promises you made and promises made to you, with due dates.
  • Tone Decoder — reads subtext and real urgency, then drafts a reply.
  • Focus Guardian — flips DND and status, and books the block.
  • Re-entry — the brief you need after time off.

Built on

Slack Real-Time Search API (assistant.search.context, permission-aware, user-token) · MCP in both directions — Tempo is itself an MCP server at /api/mcp/server · Slack AI & agent surfaces (agent_view, App Home, Block Kit, Canvas, Lists).

Assistive technology for people with ADHD, executive-function differences, and anyone returning from leave. Tempo reads only what you can already see, and never stores message content.
